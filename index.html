Our Story

Born from a love
of good food

XO Boutique Bakery is a cozy boutique café in Victoria Island known for fresh pastries, indulgent desserts, and all‑day brunch favourites.

Our space was designed to feel calm, airy, and welcoming. Whether you are meeting friends for brunch, grabbing coffee before work, or enjoying dessert after dinner, XO offers a relaxed escape in the heart of Lagos.

Every item on our menu is made with intention — sourced carefully, prepared with skill, and served with warmth. From our flaky butter croissants to our signature cheesecakes, we believe food should be a moment to savour.
